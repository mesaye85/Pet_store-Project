About

The pet salon was founded in 2016 by Mary and John. They wanted their pets to have a place that was clean, safe and pet friendly. They opened the first pet salon at 211 Shirley Ave Jacksonville, FL. Pet salons became increasingly popular, so Mary and John opened three more stores in the Jacksonville area. Pet salon specializes in providing the best dog services such as grooming, daycare and nail trimming. Pet salon has the largest indoor pet boarding and daycare facilities in the east coast.
